Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)
How can I report a lost item online?
First log in to the system through the university portal. After logging in, select the option labeled “Report Lost Item.” Then, fill in all required details, including the item type, description, last known location, date and time it was lost, and upload a photo if available. Finally, submit the report and make sure to keep the reference number for tracking purposes.

What do I need to bring when collecting my lost item?
You must show student ID and proof of ownership(photos,descriptions,password of device).
 What information should I include when reporting a lost item?
  • Item name and category
  • Physical description(color,brand,size,features)
  • Last known location
  • Date and time it was lost
  • Photo of the item(optional)
How can I check the status of my lost item report?
The website will show the status as pending(no match yet,item found,ready for collection)
 How long does the university hold found items?
